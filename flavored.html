Flavoured Milk

The ‘Daily’ range includes UHT-treated flavoured milk. It was the first dairy product packaged in Tetra Pak in Sri Lanka using aseptic and UHT procedures. The product is shelf-stable for six months under ambient temperatures and is free of artificial additives or preservatives.
The Daily range of flavoured milk offers a delightful assortment of flavours to suit various palates. Daily comes in a 200 ml slim brick pack with six flavours: vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, coffee, banana and Faluda. The one-litre pack, which comes with a cap for ease of use, was introduced for the Faluda variant to cater for the higher demand of the Ramadan season. ACTIV was the latest added flavour to the Daily Brand in order to meet the increasing demand for malt-based dairy beverages among Sri Lankan consumers. The Daily range is exported to the Maldives and is mainly popular among the Maldivian youth.
Ambewela's Flavored Milk range includes UHT-treated chocolate and Vanilla milk packaged in Tetra Pak. It is available in 1ltr and 180ml packs and Pouch packs of 150ml, and the flavored range is a popular choice amongst kids.
