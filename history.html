OUR FOUNDING STORY

Lanka Milk Foods (CWE) PLC is a local brand with a proud history of over 40 years. It has been committed to providing wholesome nutrition to generations of Sri Lankans through its wide variety of products and services. For years the brand has been consistently growing to compete against global and regional giants. First established for packeting and distribution of imported milk powder, the brand has since then progressed to becoming one of the most innovative beverage and dairy product providers in the country. It is also home to the largest packing plant in South East Asia. Today, Lanka Milk Foods (CWE) PLC is more than a mere service provider, but a trusted name across the nation that transforms the lives of all its stakeholders by providing opportunities for economic growth and sustainability

OUR FOUNDING STORY

1996
Lanka Diaries (Pvt) Ltd (1996)

A subsidiary named Lanka Diaries (Pvt) Ltd was established where milk in 2001 was packed in flavored liquid milk utilizing the UHT Tetra pack technology. It is the first company in Sri Lanka to do so.

2001
The New Zealand and Ambewela Farms (2001)

The New Zealand and Ambewela farms in Nuwara Eliya were brought over from the government. These farms are among the largest farms in the country up to date.

Ambewela Products (Pvt) Ltd (2009)

A new subsidiary established under Ambewela Products (Pvt) Ltd.
