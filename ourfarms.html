OUR FARMS

Lanka Dairies Ltd. (LDL)

Production of fresh milk, non-fat milk, Daily milk, Ambewela Flavoured milk, My Juuicee products, all of which are packaged in Tetra Packs.

Ambewela Products (Pvt) Ltd. (APL)

Production of Yogurt, Cheese, Butter, and fresh milk & Ambewela Flavored milk, which are packaged in Pouch packs.
Owns/Holds the recently upgraded dairy farm complex of 300 dairy cows located in Ambewela.

Ambewela Livestock Company Ltd. (ALC)

Owns the leasehold rights to the Ambewela farm lands from the GoSL.

Pattipola Livestock Company Ltd. (PLC)

Owns the leasehold rights to the Pattipola (New Zealand) farm lands from the GoSL

United Dairies Lanka Ltd. (UDL)

Invested in state-of-the-art dairy farm complexes in ALC and PLC Farms.
Owns the leasehold rights of ALC and PLC farm lands.
Complete management of the dairy farm complexes at ALC, PLC and APL farms
