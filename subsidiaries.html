OUR SUBSIDIARIES

AMBEWELA LIVESTOCK (PVT) LTD

The Ambewela farm situated in the district of Nuwara Eliya is the core establishment under this company. It is solely responsible for supplying cow's milk exclusively to the group companies. The farm is maintained according to the highest quality standards with regards imported from New Zealand that provides superior feed to the two most productive dairy cow species in the world: Ayrshire and Friesian.

PATTIPOLA LIVESTOCK (PVT) LTD

Pattipola Livestock Private Limited operates the Pattipola Farm, more famously known as the New Zealand Farm. This farm that is also situated in Nuwara Eliya provides high quality cow milk to the group companies. Additionally, the farm attract local and foreign visitors and provides them the opportunity to experience the processes of a dairy farm through its daily excursion program. Over the years, the Pattipola Farm has become one of the key tourist attractions in the area. New Zealand Farm is open for visitors from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. on all 365 days of the year.

AMBEWELA PRODUCTS (PVT) LTD

The company is responsible for the manufacturing and distribution of top household dairy products in Sri Lanka. Its product portfolio includes well-established flavored milk (chocolate and vanilla), yogurt as well as cream, gouda, and parmesan cheese flavor range and cheese spread. The company also experienced a massive expansion program that has further amplified its capacity and strengthened its distribution chain to become more accessible to its markets.
