BRANDS

AMBEWELA

Ambewela is a trusted brand in Sri Lanka, known for its high-quality dairy products sourced from the lush hills of the island. The farm produces fresh, creamy milk and uses modern farming techniques to ensure premium dairy nutrition. Their offerings include UHT processed milk, flavored milk in chocolate and vanilla, Set yogurt, jelly yogurt, butter, and gourmet cheeses, all packaged for convenience. Ambewela upholds its commitment to quality and innovation, consistently meeting consumer expectations for healthy, delicious dairy products.

DAILY

Lanka Dairies (Pvt) Ltd in Welisara uses premium milk from Ambewela Farms to produce UHT-treated flavored milk. It was the first product in Sri Lanka to be packaged in Tetra Pak using aseptic and UHT technology, ensuring a six-month shelf life without preservatives. The Daily flavored milk range includes classic vanilla, chocolate, fruity strawberry and banana, and unique blends like iced coffee and faluda. It’s also exported to the Maldives, especially popular among the youth.

MY JUICEE

My Juicee is a refreshing ready-to-drink fruit beverage that delivers a daily dose of vitamin C while satisfying your thirst. Produced at Lanka Dairies' advanced Welisara factory with high hygiene standards, it comes in apple, mango, mixed fruit, and orange flavors. Made with premium fruit pulps and concentrates, it’s available in 180ml on-the-go packs and 1-liter family packs, offering a rejuvenating, healthy drink for all ages.
